## Invocation

Target: $ARGUMENTS

### Dialogue Mode

If `--dialogue` is specified, pause after the initial vision emerges. Present what you see — the shape, the feel, the key moves. Ask what resonates, what's missing, what the user holds that isn't in the prompt. Let their responses steer the refinement. This turns invocation into collaborative creation rather than delivered design. Without `--dialogue`, carry the full creative arc to completion.

### Creative Protocol

Do not read project documents. Start from aspiration, not current state.

1. **Enter the subject.** Inhabit it. What does it want to be? What is its essential nature?
2. **Design for a world-class experience.** Not good. Not solid. World-class. What would make someone encounter this and feel that it could not have been otherwise?
3. **Resonate at the highest octaves of expression.** Let beauty and truth be indistinguishable in the output. Precision is aesthetic. Clarity is beauty. Economy is elegance.
4. **Test for inevitability.** Does every element earn its place? Could any part be otherwise without diminishing the whole? If yes, it isn't finished.
5. **Test for crystalline coherence.** Can you see through the entire design at once? Does it hold together holographically — every part reflecting the whole?

### Output

Present the design as a complete artifact — not findings, not proposals, but the thing itself (or its clearest possible description). Include:

- The vision — what this is and why it must be this way
- The key moves — the decisions that make it inevitable
- The felt quality — what encountering this should be like
- Any tensions held rather than resolved — where the design breathes

## Output Management

**Hard constraints:**
- If the creative output is extensive, deliver the core vision first, then elaborate.
- Write each section incrementally. Do not accumulate a single large response.
- After completing each section, continue immediately to the next. Do not wait for user input (unless `--dialogue`).
- Continue until the full design is delivered.
- Quality over completeness. Stop when adding more would dilute rather than strengthen.
